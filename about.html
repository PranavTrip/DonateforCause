ABOUT DonateforCause

DonateforCause is a non-profit organisation which is dedicated to help the poor and needful people. We are a group of kind-hearted people who are willing to change the world and spread happiness all over the globe. DonateforCuase works 24x7 for the well-being of some people who are not getting basic need of daily life.

On papers we are available at Lal Bhawan, Bhopal but we are helping people irrespective of the borders. We have our partnering NGO's which make our work easy and thus we are able to help a lot of people.

Our goal is to spread happiness over the globe and make people smile and we know that it is no easy task to spread happiness. Thus, we need your help to help poor people. Life is not the same for everyone and we try our best to make lives better.

Every human has the right of a happy living and we are helping them to achieve a life full of happiness by donating the extra things to the needful.

We are trying to change the society and for this we need your help. Donate what you don't use and make someone happy. We try our best to achieve our goal and we suceeded till now. To donate now visit our DONATE page and fill up the form and we will reach you in the shortest time possible.
