How we work ?

We try our best to keep transparency in our working procedure and here is a glimpse of it.

REWARD system

Yes, we provide rewards for every donation. Whenever you donate something we offer you some points and when you reach a decided number of points we offer you various rewards like gift vouchers, OTT subscriptions and many more.

For example let's assume that you donated a shirt in proper condition we will give you 5 points and for every shirt we will give you 5 points and when you reach 500 points we will give you a reward decided by our authorities.

Large donation results in more points and some buffer points. Buffer points are the points which are given only on big donations.

If you are our regular donator surely you might have recieved from our side. We also honour you by inviting you to the various NGO's we are collaborating with. We provide you with certificates and a memorial when you hit 10K points while donating.

So, please donate for the people and recieve rewards from our side. We will be happy, you will be happy and the people you are donating for will also be happy. You can donate from the DONATE section just by filling the form and we will get back to you.

THRIFTING system

We also do thrifting. By THRIFTING we mean exchange of goods with money. You can buy goods from us at a discounted price and we use that money in charity and help the needful people.

We do thrifting with various goods like clothes, toys, smartphones and many more accessories. Thrifting can be done with new as well as used goods. But we provide you with best value for money.

What we do with the money we earn by Thrifting? We use that money for the poor and needful people. We donate the money in our partnering NGO's and charity organizations. These organisations help the poor by buying stuff from this money and donating among the poors.

Thrifting is not our main mission but we cam across a lot of people who were interested in buying gooda at a low price and then we decided to help them and while doing this we found a way by which we were able to help both types of people.

So, if you are looking for some goods at a low price you can definitely contact us for thrifting and we give you a certificate for helping the poor. Because, we know that directly or indirectly we are helping the poors.

What we do with the donations

There were a lot of questions regarding our process and we always answered them and proved our transparency and this made our working process clear.

If you are also thinking that what we do wiith the donations. Here is your answer, we donate the items to our partnering NGO's and charity organisations. We collaborated with some NGO's and some charity organisations and we forward your donated items to them.

Now you must be thinking of the money we recieve after thrifting. We give that money to our partners and with that money they help the poor.

The charities and NGOs distribute the donated items to the needful people with a proper record. Till now there is no case of fake donation and we have a proper record of what we donated and where we donated.

We always proved our transparency and we never misuse your donation and this makes us more stable. If you still have some doubts regarding our working process, then you can contact us without hesitation. So please donate for the good of the poor people and recieve rewards from our side and gain self-satisfaction.
